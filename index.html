Auto dates by month · Day/Night shift · Only key in meter reading · Decimal reading supported · Usage auto-calculated

Only key in meter reading.
Enter exactly what you see. Example: 4183.888 or 334888. Decimal part is treated as m³ decimal, not as extra cubic meter.
Total Usage = Sum of (Current Reading − Previous Reading). Example: 4183.888 − 4183.500 = 0.388 m³, not 388 m³. First record usage is shown as 0.
