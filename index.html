GM Water Meter Report App

Input format follows your Excel: duplicate date = 1st row Day Shift, 2nd row Night Shift


Operator only fills Date and water meter readings. If the same date appears twice, the first row is Day Shift and the second row is Night Shift. Usage/difference is calculated in the Summary tab as current reading minus previous reading.
